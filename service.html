We are the one stop shop for foreign investors who need to acquire suitable mining claims for outright purchase or joint venture. We paid our clients with various mine owners in different mining sectors such as Gold, Lithium, Chrome, Antimony, Mica and various other minerals. We further offer various services necessary support for foreign investors to set up solid operations in Zimbabwe.
Regulatory compliance
We assist mining companies in understanding and complying with local, national, and international laws and regulations governing mining activities.
Permitting and licensing
We help our valued clients (both locals and foreigners) navigate the complex permitting and licensing processes required for mining operations, including environmental permits and mineral rights.
Contract drafting and negotiation
We guide our client through the complex area of by drafting and reviewing contracts related to mining activities, such as joint venture agreements, mineral leases, and offtake agreements, to protect clients' interests.
Dispute resolution
We help our clients to avoid and resolve disputes through negotiation, mediation, arbitration, or litigation related to mining contracts, property rights, environmental issues, and other legal matters.
Environmental compliance
We advise our valued clients on environmental regulations and as well as assist on the development strategies to minimize environmental impact, manage waste, and comply with reclamation requirements. We prepare environmental impact assessment reports
